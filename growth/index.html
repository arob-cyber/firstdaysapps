Features

Everything you need in the consult room

📈

WHO Growth Charts

Plot weights on official sex-specific WHO weight-for-age charts. Pinch to zoom into any time window — see just the first week if that's where your data is.

⚖️

% Weight Loss Calculator

Instantly calculate percent weight loss from birth weight. Color-coded alerts at the 7% and 10% clinical thresholds, with day-by-day expected weight ranges.

🍼

Milk Transfer Calculator

Enter pre and post-feed weights in grams. Instantly see transfer in grams, oz, and mL. Log weighted feeds against individual patients.

📄

PDF Clinical Export

Generate a professional one-page clinical summary per patient — including the growth chart, full weight history, % weight loss, and weighted feed log. Share directly to your EHR.

🔒

Privacy First

Patients are identified by MRN only — no names stored. All data stays privately on your device. Built with HIPAA-conscious workflows in mind.

📱

iPhone & iPad

Optimized for both iPhone and iPad. Data syncs privately across your own devices via iCloud so you're always working with the latest information.

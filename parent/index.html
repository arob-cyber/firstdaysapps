Features

Everything new parents need to feel confident

Free 🤱

Breastfeeding Tracker

Independent left and right breast timers that track simultaneously. The app remembers which side you fed from last and tells you which side to start on — even at 3am.

Free 🍼

Bottle & Formula Log

Track breast milk, formula, or mixed feeds by volume in oz or mL. Switch between imperial and metric at any time.

Free 💧

Diaper Tracking with AAP Milestones

Log wet and dirty diapers and receive a celebration alert when your baby hits the AAP daily minimum of 6 wet diapers — one of the most important signs they're getting enough milk.

Free 🔔

Smart Reminders

Custom feeding reminder intervals that automatically reset after every logged feed. Diaper reminders every 6 hours so nothing slips through.

Free 📱

Home Screen Widget

See your baby's last feed time, last diaper, and today's feed count at a glance — without opening the app.

Free 📚

Evidence-Based Guidance

Flange sizing guides, pump setup instructions, and links to Academy of Breastfeeding Medicine protocols and AAP guidelines — all free, built right in.

Full Access 🥛

Pumping Log & Tracker

Track pumping sessions by volume and duration with a live timer. Step-by-step setup guides for 11 major breast pump brands included.

Full Access 📈

WHO & CDC Growth Charts

Plot your baby's weight against WHO percentile curves (recommended for breastfed infants) or CDC curves (formula-fed infants) — the same charts your pediatrician uses. See the trend, not just a number.

Full Access 📄

Clinical PDF Reports

Generate a professional day-by-day summary of feeding and diaper history formatted for your IBCLC or pediatrician. Share via email, AirDrop, or messages directly from the app.
